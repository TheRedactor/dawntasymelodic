Who Am I?

Hi! I'm Jasper Jiang – a passionate 13-year-old indie author and musical artist known as Dawntasy Melodic. Through my book Dawntasy: Time's True Name, I share my creative voice and invite you on an epic YA fantasy journey filled with breathtaking adventures, mind-blowing plot twists, and a realm where truth and magic collide. As the heart behind the entire Dawntasy franchise, I blend imaginative storytelling with enchanting soundtracks to create a universe that resonates with fans of fantasy books and epic sagas.
