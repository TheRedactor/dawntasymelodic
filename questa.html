Character Profile: Questa Revelate

Questa Revelate

Questa Revelate (SLCCR) is a brave human warrior who specialises in combat and inventing. He has paved the way as a leader for numerous generations of TRL members and his motivating words have enlightened/encouraged those who follow him.

Background

As the half brother of SLCCR Constellation Filapawa, Questa is a brave and kind-hearted man who fights for the revolution. As a child, Questa demonstrated excellent abilities in combat and took serious attention to leadership courses.
