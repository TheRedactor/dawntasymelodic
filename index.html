Welcome to the world of Gaze!

Dawntasy Story is an unpublished saga revolving around fantasy and dystopian elements. Delve into the Gaze world in an era in which survival is not guaranteed, and happiness is scarce. The outlook of the health of the future world does not prove to be promising, and as a band of desperate mutineers cling onto the threads of dear life, they realise that not just them are in danger, the whole world is, the whole multiverse.

A tale of adventure and courage...

About the World of Gaze

The world of Gaze is split into two realms: the Rift Realm and the Dawnstory Realm. The Rift Realm is a dangerous place filled with magic and fantasy, while the Dawnstory Realm is a typical fantasy world. Both realms are surrounded by celestial stars and constellations, making the world of Gaze a unique and fantastical place.

Featured Story: His Promise (S1B1)

The realm of Dawnstory is governed by a tyrannous, nefarious organisation known only as LTR. When all hell breaks loose, the Time Rebellion League is formed with only one obligation, taking down the organisation and its devious leader, while Yaya the bearman struggles to find peace within himself after his beloved master is slain by enemy forces. However, he begins to realise that all is not lost, and that backing down was the worst thing he could have done.

The Heroes of Dawnstory!

Yaya Ursa

Yaya Ursa is a bearman from the bear tribe who wields a pike. He has gone through numerous traumas such as losing his family and Aegis Wick, which has hindered his growth and caused him to think negatively of the world.
Questa Revelate

Questa Revelate is a brave human swordsman who wields a sword and a cape, often depicted in a heroic stance. When Time Smith rose to power, he was separated from his brother and has been fighting for freedom ever since.
Filapawa Revelate

Filapawa Revelate is a herbal shaman in the form of an elemental bird with the Libra constellation sign resonating as him. As one of the celestial Constellation Tempests, Filapawa possesses the duty to guard the world.
Norsty Tabla

Norsty Tabla is a swordsman of celestial might, depicted with the Aries constellation, with the laws of harmony binding him to his position as a Tempest. However, his defeat lead him to darkness and twisted beliefs.
Dryadis Petella

Dryadis Petella is a beautiful maiden dryad with roots, tree, and flowers blooming around her.
Salvapige Invicton

Salvapige Invicton is a brave warrior with a big cape. He is renowned as a venerable hero for slaying enemies and being "unstoppable".
Solus Oculte

Solus Oculte is an arcane wizard with dark powers, typically depicted with a hood that obscures his eyes and his staff raised, emitting dark powers. He befriends Yaya and escapes his solitude, resulting in an endless adventure.
