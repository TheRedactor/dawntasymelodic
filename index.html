Dawntasy (or Fantasy and the World Work Dictionary & Thesaurus With Companion Story) is an unpublished saga revolving around fantasy and dystopian elements. About the World of Dawntasy

The world of Dawntasy is split into two realms: the Rift Realm and the Dawnstory Realm. The Rift Realm is a dangerous place filled with magic and fantasy, while the Dawnstory Realm is a typical fantasy world. Both realms are surrounded by celestial stars and constellations, making the world of Gaze a unique and fantastical place.

Featured Story: His Promise (S1B1)

The realm of Dawnstory is governed by a tyrannous, nefarious organisation known only as LTR. When all hell breaks loose, the Time Rebellion League is formed with only one obligation, taking down the organisation and its devious leader, while Yaya the bearman struggles to find peace within himself after his beloved master is slain by enemy forces. However, he begins to realise that all is not lost, and that backing down was the worst thing he could have done.
