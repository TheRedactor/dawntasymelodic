Character Profile: Yaya Ursa

Yaya Ursa

Yaya Ursa is a bearman from the bear tribe who wields a pike. He has gone through numerous traumas such as losing his family and Aegis Wick, which has hindered his growth and caused him to think negatively of the world. However, he begins to realise that all is not lost, and that backing down was the worst thing he could have done.

Background

Yaya's story is one of tragedy and resilience. Despite the many hardships he has faced, he remains a loyal and brave warrior, determined to find peace within himself and fight for justice in the world of Dawntasy.

Abilities

  • Pike Mastery: Expert in using his pike in combat, both offensively and defensively.
  • Bear Strength: Possesses immense physical strength, making him a formidable opponent.
  • Resilience: Highly resilient to physical and emotional pain, driven by his past traumas.
