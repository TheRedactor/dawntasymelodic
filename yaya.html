Character Profile: Yaya Ursa

Yaya Ursa

Yaya Ursa is a bearman from the bear tribe who wields a pike. He has gone through numerous traumas such as losing his family and Aegis Wick, which has hindered his growth and caused him to think negatively of the world. As the series goes on, he begins to realise the multifaceted reality of the world and proceeds his adventure on a victorious note.

Background

Yaya's story is one of tragedy and resilience. Despite the many hardships he has faced, he remains a loyal and brave warrior, determined to find peace within himself and fight for justice in the world of Dawntasy. When he was very little, his parents were killed, and his bear community was obliterated to rubble...and his happiness. That was when he was taken into the care and guidance of Aegis Wick. After being mentored for two decades, Aegis Wick met his untimely end, in which Yaya was forced to pursue his own dreams without Aegis, to step up into the light.
