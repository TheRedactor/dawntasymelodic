Evolution

The Journey of DawntasyAI

Trace the evolution of our AI companion from concept to reality, paralleling the journey of Yaee as he discovers the truth about The Rift.

PHASE ONE

Conceptual Dawn

The initial concept of DawntasyAI emerged from the desire to extend the universe of "Time's True Name" into an interactive experience, allowing readers to engage directly with the narrative.

PHASE TWO

Prototype Development

Early prototypes focused on integrating the lore and themes of Dawntasy, creating a foundation for an AI companion that understood the nuances of the universe.

PHASE THREE

The ARCHMAGE Integration

Development of the ARCHMAGE Tool, which enabled the AI to analyze topics from multiple perspectives, mirroring the complex understanding of reality presented in the book.

PHASE FOUR

Memory Code Implementation

Creation of the Memory Code system, allowing the AI to maintain continuity across conversations, reflecting the cyclical nature of time in the Dawntasy universe.

PRESENT

The Circular Dawn

DawntasyAI now stands as a fully realized companion, offering insights into the universe of "Time's True Name" and extending the experience beyond the pages of the book.
